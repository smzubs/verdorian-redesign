### 2026 fresh findings on "why choose us" conversion

**Finding 1: "Why choose us" sections fail when they're generic. They work when they're specific and falsifiable.**

Source: digitalapplied.com, searchenginejournal.com (fetched 2026-06-23)

Surface signals — badges, adjectives ("passionate," "innovative," "experienced"), vague guarantees — no longer move the needle. What converts in 2026:
- Named, quantified case studies with specific outcomes.
- Honest acknowledgment of limitations alongside strengths.
- A specific, operational guarantee placed directly below the CTA.
- Technical transparency: showing the method, not just the result.

**Verdict for Verdorian:** The existing 5 angles in content-spec-v2.md are well-written, but they work best when each one has a PROOF ELEMENT attached — not just a warm sentence, but a link or reference to the concrete reality. Example: "We've actually done the work" → immediately followed by a reference to QRSafePro (live link). **Fact (pattern, high confidence).**
